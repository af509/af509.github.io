Our Story

In the summer of 2023, I rescued a dog from a hoarding situation. There was over 55 dogs located in one house that were not discovered until the owner passed away. Rescues all across Michigan worked together to help the dogs. These actions inspired me to want to help dogs in any way I can, plus who wouldn't want to spend all day with precious doggies.
